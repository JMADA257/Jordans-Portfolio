About me

Hello, thanks for coming to my portfolio! My name is Jordan Adams, and im currently in the process of becoming a web developer. Originally I came from a small state, Delaware. at 18 years i finished high school and went into the United States Marine Corps as a tank operator. I did this for about 3 years before I got out. Pretty awesome thing to be able to shoot tanks for a job! After that I went back home for a short while before heading to Colorado to work at the Ritz-Carlton as a server! I ended up working there for about 3 years before I left and became lead bartender at Fattoria restaurant, a multi million dollar locally owned business! I was in charge of creating the new cocktail menu and making sure the bar was kept like a well oiled machine. Finally I finished working there after about a year to head to Denver to attend the course im currently in for website development! Thats about it for me!
